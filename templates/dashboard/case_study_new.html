Writing Guide

  • 1 Name characters — Indian first names (Rahul, Priya, Arjun) make the scenario feel authentic and relatable.
  • 2 Set up a real tension — The student should face a genuine dilemma, not an obvious right answer.
  • 3 Show, don't tell — Describe what happens and what characters say; let the student infer feelings.
  • 4 End mid-dilemma — Stop before the resolution so the AI can explore possibilities with the student.
  • 5 Probing angles — Ask about values, fears, relationships, and consequences — not just facts.
